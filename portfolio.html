About

I'm an ambitious front-end developer, with experience in QA Testing. My main focus lies in Javascript and SCSS, and I'm starting to dive into Tailwinds and Vue. Recently I created a small project with Tailwinds and greatly enjoyed how easy it was to build a project up from scratch.

With a Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Technology from BYU-I, I am currently a Manual QA Tester at Melaleuca, and have been with them for over 4 years.

Hours

While working full time, I still find the time to code.

I'm a firm believer in Malcom Gladwell's 10,000 hours ideology and aspire to hit that benchmark. In fact I log my hours whenever I code so that I can make that dream a reality.

My time coding is spent either on projects, specifically new topics I'm learning, or various algorithm challenges.

Projects

In an effort to solidify my understanding of the foundations, I have a number of projects that utilize HTML, CSS, and JS.

To stay relevant and increase my awareness of the ever-changing field in IT, I have expanded beyond the core three and have recently worked with Tailwind Css, API's, user scripts, and even SQL. My learning is never complete. While I have not yet built a project in a JS framework, I have chosen as Vue as my starting point and I'm excited to see where it takes me.
